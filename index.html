Grace Kim

UX/UR designer

I have always been a very curious person growing up, and as a UX/UI designer I love being able to take that curiosity and listen to and learn about people in order to create creative, innovative design solutions.

My education in statistics and media studies helped me discover my interest in collecting and analyzing data and the importance of effectively conveying the story behind that data.

I was born and raised in Northern Virginia - just 20 minutes outside of Washington D.C. Through my love for travel, I have been visited many parts of the world beyond Virginia to experience different cultures through experiences, people, and food!

Thank you for getting to know me and my work! I am currently looking for new opportunities, and would love to hear from you about any positions or just to chat!
